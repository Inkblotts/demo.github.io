WHO IS SHE?


Flanked by mountains, on one side
Cut by water, stands in pride

Soaked in blood lies her heart

Yet her children move apart

They have grown like loveless thorns

Her each fragment—a corpse adorns

Poverty builds up her tagline

Violence causes her decline

Dark woods carry martyrs’ song

Bells of liberty—did they gong?

The soldiers ask, via nature’s media

Is she the same, Republic India?

Manjima Ghosh
MEGHNAD SAHA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY
